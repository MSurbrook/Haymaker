HAYMAKER!

An APAzine for the HERO System.

Welcome to Haymaker!

Haymaker! is an Amateur Press Association magazine (APAzine) supporting the HERO System (a multi-genre role-playing game). This small press production was founded in 1992 and is currently distributed quarterly in electronic format to all contributors. Please note that issues of Haymaker! are not available for subscription or sale to the general public.

If you're interested in writing and becoming a contributor to Haymaker!, please read our Contributor's Guidelines and contact our coordinator. We're also looking for good artists to provide cover art; again, contact our coordinator if you're interested.

Even if neither of these applies to you, please feel free to follow the links on top to learn more about Haymaker!, check out the covers and contents of our past issues, and see other sites related to Hero Games or other topics of interest to gamers.
